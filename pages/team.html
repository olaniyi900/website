About Us

Who are we

Minds & Souls Foundation is an NGO whose goal
is to eradicate illiteracy in Africa- one child,
one community and one country at a time.

We provide a donation platform, in addition to
humanitarian support, to assist in providing
basic schooling amenities to educate
the African child.
